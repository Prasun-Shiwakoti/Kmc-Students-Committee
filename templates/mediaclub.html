Introducing Media Club


"What separates good content from great content is a willingness to take risks and push the envelope".

Welcome to KMC media club. The KMC Media Club is made up of a close-knitted group of learners who share a common interest in photography and live sound production. They work tirelessly around the institute to support all it's visual and media needs throughout the year.

The students serve the institue in many areas, ranging from playing a pivotal role in ensuring the success of every major events and concerts. They also capture the important moments in these events for the purpose of sharing on media platforms and keeping these precious moments for future publications.

# OBJECTIVES OF COMPUTER CLUB
1 Organizing events and competitions to enhance creativity.
2 Documenting various projects through photos and videos.
3 Create graphic contents and notices.
4 Displaying the creativity of learners and support them.
