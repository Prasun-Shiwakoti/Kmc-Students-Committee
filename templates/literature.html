Introducing Literature Club


“Since soul is our author, we live immortal”


Literature club of Kathmandu Model College Student’s Committee, functioning under the alias “The Wordsworthers” formulated in 2017 A.D. is an asylum for the literary minds to pour out the literary skill that lives concealed within themselves. We try to work against the stereotype with the activities we perform. We believe that literature is a voice to many and we respect the voice of everyone as we believe that the voice is kingpin of our club. Hailing from different backgrounds, places, situations and mindset, the members of the club work in accordance to each other’s strengths and weaknesses and try to convert our weaknesses to power with the flare of teamwork.

# OBJECTIVES OF o Literature club
1 1. It provides a platform for those who deserve their voice to be heard, we don’t have preference towards membership of any kind, we all are just admirers of a beautiful complex called “Literature”
2 2. We believe in “Expression over Impression”
3 3. To embed the feeling of teamwork within the members and help them develop oratorial skills.
4 4. Participation in intra-college and inter-college level programs related to literature.
5 5. Working on development of a reading-writing culture in the vicinity of KMC.
6 6. Emphasizing on free and profound writing rather than bounded writing.
