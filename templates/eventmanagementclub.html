Introducing Evemt Management Club


"When there is teamwork and collaboration wonderful things can be achieved."

You know the relation between the pencil, eraser, and sharpener, right? The eraser erasens the mistake of pencil and sharpener sharpens and energizes the blunt pencil. Have you ever imagined a team working with such understanding? If not, we welcome you to EMC where every member corrects the mistakes and energizes each other with a dedication. It is a club which believes in the magic of co-ordination and teamwork, a club whose strength is its individual members and individual member's strength is the club itself.

Students here are learning the essence of teamwork where creating memory and fun goes hand in hand. Managing an event without any flaws has been the indispensable part of EMC. So, with every individual's creativity and ideas. We are moving further.

# OBJECTIVES OF Event Management Club (EMC)
1 We help to manage the overall activities that are conducted during an event to make it efficient and successful.
2 We help to eliminate the errors and flaws of the respective event.
3 We try to conclude the event within the time and exclude the delay.
4 We support other clubs during their event and activities to obtain the best result.
5 We present new and innovative ideas to the executive body for the betterment of the committee and students of KMC.
