Introducing Arts & Culture Club


"Create with your heart, build with your mind"

A club with the imagination, creativeness and a something of craziness flowing through the heart and mind of all the members. A club that shares the unique and different prospective toward art and culture. Our club previously just known as art club became ART AND CULTURE club with the believe that culture is arts evaluated to set off believe. Our club wants to look the world with our own artistic way and vision. Each and every member of the club are willing to learn something new that beholds our heart. We are doing what we wished for, learning something we really want to. We do our work with everything we got, trying to make it best. We create wonderful memories here with everyone that could be shared and remembered. The club members are best at their own works. We work together to light up the programs that are conducted and make it more admirable through our work. Never or ever just show up your heart.

# OBJECTIVES OF Art And Culture Club
1 To create the club where one is unbound of limits.
2 TO organize events and competitions to enhance art and culture.
3 To decorate each and every programs to make it success.
4 To help others to display their creativity and learns as they wish to.
5 Motivating and Uplifting the members to prosper in computer skill
