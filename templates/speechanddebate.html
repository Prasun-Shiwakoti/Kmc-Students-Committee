Introducing Arts & Culture Club


The speech and debate club is devoted to advance and advocate public speaking within and outside the college vicinity and is committed to develop oratory skills of general KMCians.

Speech and Debate club is a club that works under the supervision of KMC Students Committee. It is a group of voracious orators who work as a team to organize activities related to speech and debate within and around the college premises. This club organizes speech and debate competitions throughout the year for general KMCians and extends invitation to other institutions as well. This club is dedicated to promote public speaking and representing KMC in intercollege debates. The members work together to cherish and flourish oratory skills of students of KMC by encouraging them to take part in different events related to public speaking.
