Introducing Music Club


"Music speaks there, where words can't"

The Music Club aims to promote taste and talent amongst KMCSS students by organizing competitions, programs and performances. It offers an opportunity for students to come together and discuss music and a chance to perform and showcase their talents at events throughout the year. The club emphasizes on all types of talents, hence has allowed students from various spheres of music(both vocatis and instruments)to come together and work in harmony for better outcomes throughout the year in the competitions and programmes. Apart from these, meetings are scheduled based on the activities that should be carried on by our club in the callege programs and student committee throughout the year.

Goal Of The Club


KMC Music Club focuses on cultivation of musical talent around the college. Students get to participate and exposed to various musical activities throughout the academic year. They are highly encouraged to develop and acquire musical skills. KMC Music Club is a precious part of the Student Committee, where the musical talent grows and where the leadership skills gets practiced and encouraged in club meetings.
