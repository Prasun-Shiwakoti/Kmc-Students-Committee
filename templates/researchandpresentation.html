Introducing Research & Presentation Club


TO CREATE “HOW” AND ANSWER “WHY”

“Research is formalized curiosity. It is poking and prying with a purpose”. Our team is a collection of bright-minded people who share a common interest and are ready to tackle questions and provide you with its answer

Welcome to the “KMC Research and Presentation Club”. Finding new things is always fun and we as a Club find various research questions and try to answer them in a unique way. We encourage the curiosity and enthusiasm of students who are trying to find unique answers. We believe every question has a purpose and that there is always an answer which we try to uncover. We aim to develop basic research skills in students so that they can go through different sources to get conclusions & develop a research environment at a young age.

Our club helps increase the critical thinking of students and make the students more innovative by generating creative & new ideas.

# OBJECTIVES OF RESEARCH AND PRESENTATION CLUB
1 To create a path for generating new questions.
2 To enhance the analytical nature of members.
3 To conclude concerns
4 To assist the committee to be presentable.
