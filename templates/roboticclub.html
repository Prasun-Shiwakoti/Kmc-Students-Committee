Introducing Robotics Club


"One Team One Robot Unlimited possibilities"

The Robotics club of Kathmandu Model Secondary school is a student-run club founded by energetic and enthusiastic seniors solely out of passion for Robotics. Robotics Club has been the harbinger in bringing the knowledge and technology of Robotics in whole of KMC.

The Club has been involved in a continuous process of learning and exploring the possibilities in the field of robotics most of which comes from the hands-on knowledge and experience in building robots. We have designed and built various robots, all which have represented Kathmandu Model College in various competitions.

# OBJECTIVES OF ROBOTICS CLUB
1 To facilitate students to understand, design and learn robotics.
2 To provide interested students with opportunities to express their skills, knowledge and creativity through conceptualizing, designing and programming robots.
3 To encourage and enable students to utilize and enrich their creativity and engineering skills through robotics.
4 To imbibe technical skills, teamwork and leadership in the interested youths.
5 To inspire young minds to be the driving force in the field of robotics, by engaging them in exciting, mentor-based programs that build engineering skills, inspire innovation, and foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication and leadership.
