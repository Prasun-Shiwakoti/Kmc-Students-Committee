Introducing Legal Club


“Ethic is knowing the difference between what you have a right to do and what is right to do”

Welcome to KMC Legal club. The KMC Legal club is made up of an energetic, friendly, innovative and fresh learner whose joint goal is to protect the constitution of KMC student committee. It is a club having interactive group of Legal learners with the same thirst for extra knowledge of researching new topics and sharing the same ideas. This club is filled with members who have interest in reading legal articles, new case laws and Researching on different legal topics. This club member works tirelessly and Conducts research to ensure that the current articles mentioned in the constitution is enough to address the current problem in the committee throughout the year. The members of legal club work for the betterment of all the student of KMC and institution itself. Bound everyone in systematic rules and regulation and instruct everyone that “ignorance of law is not excused.”

“justice that love gives is a surrender, justice that law gives are a punishment.”

The legal club holds many responsibilities as being the part of this club duties ranges from supervising the activities of other club committee activities as provided in constitution, to bringing different drafting proposals to amend or repeal the current existing KMC students committee to ensure the current articles mentioned in Constitution are enough to address problems. This legal club also ensures that the articles mentioned in the KMC student constitution is being implemented properly, and also conducts different programs with the collaboration with other clubs creating a beautiful moment between club members.

# OBJECTIVES OF Legal Club
1 Organizing moot courts and also conducting different speech and debate competitions on legal topics with the collaboration with others clubs to enhance leadership, creativity, and confidence.
2 To supervise and to ensure the members of the committee along with club heads and executive members are following the current constitution of the KMC student committee.
3 To bound everyone in a rules and regulation.
